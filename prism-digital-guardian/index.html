04 — Bluetooth Proximity Intelligence

Who Was
Near Her?

Every person enrolled in PRISM Digital Guardian becomes a passive sensor node in a state-wide proximity network. When someone goes missing, the system answers the hardest investigative question — who was physically near the victim, when, and where — even in areas with zero CCTV coverage, zero witnesses, and total darkness.

Network Effect — Coverage Grows with Adoption
More enrolled users = broader investigative coverage across the state
How the Proximity Log Works — 3 Steps
Same architecture as Aarogya Setu contact tracing — proven at 50M+ scale in India
STEP 01 — BROADCAST (Always On)
Each PRISM app device broadcasts rotating encrypted proximity tokens via Bluetooth Low Energy Advertisement — same proven architecture as Aarogya Setu (50M+ users, India-scale validated).
STEP 02 — SCAN + LOG (Locally Encrypted on Device)
Simultaneously scans for nearby tokens in balanced mode (2s scan, 3s rest). Logs token + signal strength + GPS + timestamp locally in AES-256 encrypted database. No data leaves the device until police requisition.
STEP 03 — CORRELATE (PRISM Server · Police Access Only)
On complaint, victim's proximity log uploads to PRISM server. Server cross-correlates tokens against all enrolled users. Output: a verified timeline of who was near the victim, when, where, at what approximate distance. Only authorised PRISM officers can trigger this.
Battery Impact — Full PRISM Monitoring Stack
Designed to be acceptable on standard Indian market phones (3,500–5,000 mAh)
Aarogya Setu Proof
India already accepted BLE proximity tracing at 50M+ users
The identical BLE token-broadcast architecture was used at national scale during COVID. India's public is familiar and accepting of proximity-based safety apps. PRISM applies the same technology to crime prevention — with stronger privacy controls. Only police can access data.
MAC Randomisation
Android 10+ rotates Bluetooth address every 10–15 minutes — bypassed by app tokens
The cooperative token model bypasses MAC randomisation entirely. App-level encrypted tokens are persistent regardless of MAC rotation. Non-enrolled devices are still fingerprinted by manufacturer ID (Apple, Samsung, Google) and non-randomising accessories like smartwatches and earbuds.
Where CCTV Cannot Reach
Rural AP, tribal areas, alleys, homes — where surveillance has no reach
CCTV covers main roads and public spaces in urban areas. BLE proximity works in homes, alleys, agricultural land, tribal villages — wherever two enrolled phones are present. This is the only technology that establishes proximity evidence in rural Andhra Pradesh where no other surveillance infrastructure exists.
