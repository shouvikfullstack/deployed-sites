Section 01

VAPI.AI — Powerful but
Built for America

VAPI is a developer-first voice AI orchestration platform. It coordinates three pipeline modules — Transcriber (STT), Model (LLM), and Voice (TTS) — processing audio in 20ms chunks with streaming at every layer. Partial STT results feed immediately into the LLM, which starts generating before the user even finishes speaking.

✅ Where VAPI Shines

  • Best-in-class developer flexibility — mix any provider
  • ~465ms latency achievable (US-based deployments)
  • Proprietary interruption handling (not just silence detection)
  • Wide LLM support: GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, Groq, Mistral
  • Function calling, webhooks, Make/GoHighLevel integrations
  • Active developer community & good documentation

❌ Critical India Failures

  • >1,500ms latency from India (us-west-2 servers only)
  • Zero Hinglish / code-switching support
  • No Indian phone numbers natively (US numbers only)
  • No TRAI/DLT compliance features built in
  • No Indian accent training in STT models
  • Trustpilot rating: 2.6/5 — pricing complaints dominant

Indian Language Support —
What Actually Works

🎤

STT (Speech → Text)

Sarvam Saaras v3 — Clear winner. 19.31% WER on IndicVoices, covers all 22 scheduled languages, sub-150ms TTFT. Trained on 1M+ hours including real Hinglish conversations.

AI4Bharat IndicWhisper — Best open-source option. Lowest WER on 39/59 Vistaar benchmarks, MIT license, 12 languages. One model per language, no native code-switching.

Google Cloud STT — Broadest coverage but 23.9% WER on Hindi benchmarks. Inconsistent on accents.

Deepgram Nova-3 — Supports Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi but outputs mixed-script on code-switched audio.

🔊

TTS (Text → Speech)

Sarvam Bulbul v3 — 35+ voices, 10 Indian languages, native Hinglish code-switching. Correct pronunciation of Lajpat Nagar, Koramangala etc. ₹30/10K characters.

AI4Bharat Indic-TTS — Open source, 13 languages (FastPitch + HiFi-GAN). Indic Parler-TTS supports 21 languages with style prompts.

Google/Azure Neural TTS — Decent Indian voices at $13–16/M characters. Generic quality, not India-optimised.

ElevenLabs — Only Hindi & Tamil supported. Avoid for multilingual Indian deployments.

⚡

The Hinglish / Code-Switching Problem

~250 million Indians speak code-switched (Hinglish) daily. Standard ASR models see a 30–50% WER increase on code-switched speech. A sentence like "I want to check my balance aur ek cheque deposit bhi karna hai" — that's everyday India.

SARVAM AI ✅
Treats code-mixing as primary architectural feature. mode="codemix" maintains entity recognition across language switches. Currently irreplaceable.
DEEPGRAM ⚠
Can transcribe code-switched audio but outputs English in Latin script + Hindi in Devanagari — mixed-script mess.
GOOGLE / AZURE / WHISPER ❌
All struggle significantly with mid-sentence language switching. Not production-ready for India.

Section 06

How to Deploy —
End to End Guide

01

Choose Stack by Scale

Under 10K calls/month: Bolna.dev (India) or Retell (English) + Sarvam STT/TTS + Exotel. Over 10K calls: Build custom orchestration on Exotel's WebSocket streaming API connecting your own STT→LLM→TTS pipeline directly.
02

Telephony & TRAI Compliance

DLT registration with telecom operators is mandatory for outbound. Use 140-series numbers for promotional, 1600-series for transactional. NDNC scrub before every dial. Penalty: up to ₹10 lakh per instance. February 2025: consent validity now 7 days only.
03

Build a Context Agent, Not IVR

RAG: Connect LLM to a vector DB (Pinecone/pgvector) with your product docs. System prompts: Define persona, escalation triggers, compliance rules, code-switching instructions. Function calling: CRM updates, calendar booking, payment links. Memory: Maintain 10–15 turn sliding window + extracted entities.
04

Voice + WhatsApp Hybrid

Most effective Indian architecture. After voice call, trigger a webhook → send WhatsApp with summary, payment link, or appointment confirmation via Exotel or Gupshup WhatsApp Business API. This hybrid approach increases completion rates by 40–60% versus voice-only.
05

CRM & Webhook Architecture

call.started → log to CRM → call.ended → push transcript + extracted entities + recording URL. Use Make.com or n8n (self-hosted, free) for complex multi-step webhook orchestration.
06

Test Before You Launch

Test 50+ scenarios per language — background noise, children crying, bad signal, code-switching, Aadhaar/PAN capture, escalation triggers. Target 95%+ intent accuracy per language before go-live. Use native speakers, not translations. Run weekly regression tests after any model update.

India Compliance Summary

TRAI — Marketing Calls

140-series numbers only. NDNC scrub mandatory. Calling hours: 9 AM–9 PM only. DLT registration required for all commercial communications.

RBI — Debt Collection

Calling hours: 8 AM–7 PM only. No threatening or coercive language. No disclosure to third parties. All calls must be recorded and logged.

RBI — Digital Lending

Explicit consent recording mandatory. PAN/Aadhaar handling subject to data localisation rules. High STT accuracy critical for compliance record-keeping.

DPDPA 2023

Data Principal consent required before processing personal data. Purpose limitation applies. Cross-border transfer restrictions for sensitive categories.

⚠️ BPO Disruption: The Warning India Cannot Ignore

Gartner forecasts conversational AI will cut global contact center labor costs by $80 billion by 2026. McKinsey estimates GenAI could automate 30% of Indian customer operations hours near-term. NASSCOM data: 640,000 low-skilled service jobs face displacement against only 160,000 mid-to-high-skill roles being created. The Indian BPO industry's competitive advantage — low wages, English-trained graduates — is precisely what voice AI replicates most easily. Voice-based services represent 35% of BPO revenue and face the highest automation exposure. Kolkata and Jaipur centers serving domestic BFSI processes are most immediately at risk.

New roles emerging: AI trainers (₹4–8 LPA), prompt engineers (₹6–15 LPA), conversation designers (₹5–10 LPA), AI quality analysts, orchestration engineers — but net job displacement is negative in the near term.

New Client Onboarding — Step by Step

Step 01
Discovery & Scoping
1–2 weeks
Collect existing call transcripts and agent scripts. Define use cases, call volumes per day, languages spoken by callers, current CRM system and its API maturity. Map compliance requirements (RBI for BFSI, TRAI for outbound, DPDP Act for data handling). Define escalation triggers and KPIs. Critical output: a signed-off conversation flow diagram, integration list, and compliance checklist before a single line of code is written.
Step 02 — ⚠️ START FROM DAY 1 IN PARALLEL — DO NOT DELAY
DLT Registration — The Biggest Timeline Blocker
2–6 weeks (blocks go-live if delayed)
TRAI mandates DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) registration before any outbound commercial voice calls. Must register separately on Airtel, Jio, Vi, and BSNL portals for full nationwide coverage. Entity registration: 3–7 business days per operator. Header (Sender ID) registration: additional 3–7 days per operator. One-time cost: approximately ₹5,900 incl. GST. Promotional calls must use 140-series numbers. Transactional/service calls use 1600-series. Mandatory AI voice disclosure at call start (per TRAI Feb 2025 amendment). Start this on Day 1 — it blocks your entire go-live if delayed.
Step 03
Prompt Engineering & Conversation Design
2–4 weeks
Convert existing call scripts into voice-specific AI prompts. Voice prompting is fundamentally different from text: short sentences, no markdown, natural Hinglish code-switching, interruption handling, real-time uncertainty management. Design the AI persona (name, tone, escalation language). Map 50+ edge cases including rage quits, garbled audio, topic drift. Hard-code all compliance guardrails outside the LLM — never trust the LLM to generate regulatory disclosures, legal disclaimers, or compliance scripts. These must be static strings. Expect 3–5 rounds of iteration before the conversation feels natural to native speakers.
Step 04
Technical Integration
1–3 weeks
Set up API keys and credentials across Sarvam (STT + TTS), Bolna (orchestration), and Exotel (telephony). Provision Indian phone numbers — Exotel KYC verification required before streaming is enabled (potential 1–2 week delay). CRM webhook architecture: call.started → log metadata to CRM → during call, function calls extract structured data → call.ended → push full transcript + extracted entities + recording URL to CRM. WhatsApp Business API integration via Exotel or Gupshup for post-call follow-ups. Use Make.com (for no-code) or n8n (self-hosted, free) for multi-step webhook workflows. Verify Bolna-Exotel integration is confirmed as production-ready before finalising architecture — listed as "coming soon" in public docs as of March 2026.
Step 05
QA & Testing
1–2 weeks
Build a test suite of 50+ scenarios per language. Include real-world edge cases: heavy background noise (market, traffic), children crying in background, poor mobile signal, mid-sentence code-switching, numeric capture (Aadhaar, PAN, salary amounts), multiple-topic digressions, and explicit compliance trigger phrases. Test with native speakers from target dialects — not just city-educated speakers. Load test to 10–30 concurrent calls. Run compliance script review with legal/compliance team. Target 95%+ intent accuracy per language before go-live. All test calls should be recorded for manual spot-audit.

1–2 weeks soft + ongoing iteration
Route 5–10% of live traffic to the AI agent first. Monitor five KPIs weekly: STT accuracy per language, intent recognition rate, call completion rate, escalation rate, and average handle time. Iterate on prompts based on real conversation data — this is ongoing, not one-time. After 2 consecutive stable weeks: ramp to full traffic. Failure Modes & How to Prevent Them

🎤 STT Accuracy Degrades in Production

Street noise, crowds, children crying, or poor mobile signal can degrade accuracy by 30%+. The 8kHz PSTN audio quality vs. 16kHz lab test conditions is a permanent gap. Dravidian languages start at 15–20% WER baseline before noise.

✅ Use Sarvam for all Indian language calls — 5–6× better than global platforms on Hindi/Bengali. Add automatic gain control on the audio stream. Set confidence threshold escalation: if STT confidence <0.6 on critical data capture, repeat or escalate to human. Always test with real mobile recordings from target geography, not studio samples.

🧠 LLM Hallucination in Compliance Contexts

An LLM fabricating a payment amount, wrong EMI date, or incorrect regulatory disclosure in a collections or lending call creates immediate RBI/TRAI violation and legal liability. Hallucination risk is highest when LLM is asked to recall specific policy details from memory.

✅ RAG with a verified, human-reviewed knowledge base reduces hallucinations by ~71%. Hard-code ALL compliance disclosures, legal disclaimers, and consent scripts as static strings outside the LLM — never generated. Use GPT-4.1 Structured Outputs for any financial data extraction (near-100% schema compliance). Avoid reasoning models (o3, o4-mini) — they hallucinate more than standard models in constrained tasks.

📞 Telephony Outage — Platform Down

Exotel attempts one automatic WebSocket retry on connection failure. Extended outages (minutes+) have no built-in fallback. Full platform downtime means all concurrent calls fail simultaneously.

✅ Build an explicit fallback chain in Exotel's applet config: WebSocket AI Agent → on failure → traditional IVR (pre-recorded) → on failure → human agent queue → on failure → call-back scheduling. Set up monitoring alerts via Pagerduty/Slack within 60 seconds of failure. Maintain a small human agent reserve during peak hours as immediate backup capacity.

⚖️ Regulatory Violation — TRAI / RBI / DPDP

Missing mandatory AI disclosure at call start. Dialling outside permitted hours. Calling a DND-registered number. Storing call recordings without proper consent. TRAI penalty: up to ₹10 lakh per instance + 2-year blacklisting. DPDP Act (effective 2027): up to ₹250 crore per breach.

✅ AI voice disclosure must be the literal first utterance of every call (not optional). Scrub every dial list against NDNC registry before each batch. Time-gate all outbound calls: TRAI marketing = 9AM–9PM only; RBI collections = 8AM–7PM only. Store call recordings minimum 1 year with encrypted PII. Treat compliance as a hard-coded layer, never a prompt instruction.
